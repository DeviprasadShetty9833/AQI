Our Mission

Demystifying Environmental Health

Our mission is to empower communities, policymakers, and individuals with clear, accurate, and real-time environmental information. We believe that by translating complex data into intuitive indices like the Air Quality Index (AQI) and our innovative Noise Pollution Index, we can foster a deeper understanding of our environment and drive positive change.

Our Focus

Monitoring Air Quality and Urban Soundscapes

Air Quality Index (AQI)

The AQI is your guide to local air quality. It measures major pollutants like particulate matter (PM2.5, PM10), ozone, and carbon monoxide to help you make informed decisions for your well-being.

Noise Pollution Index

Our innovative Noise Pollution Index tracks urban sound levels in decibels. This data is vital for urban planning and creating healthier, more peaceful living environments for everyone.
